    * Room --> rent

    * focus on application instead of infra

    * no maintainees

    * pay-as-you-go
    * can access remotely/internet
    * email,google-drive,netflix examples of cloud. 
    * scalable on-the-fly -- based on traffic
    * scale down --> traffic low
